## üîß TECHNICAL MODIFIERS

### **Quality Levels**
- `4K ultra HD` - Highest quality, slower generation
- `1080p HD` - Standard quality, good balance
- `720p` - Faster generation, lower quality

### **Frame Rates**
- `24fps` - Cinematic feel
- `30fps` - Standard video
- `60fps` - Smooth motion, gaming content

### **Camera Movements**
- `Static shot` - No movement
- `Slow pan` - Horizontal movement
- `Dolly zoom` - Push in/pull out
- `Orbit` - 360-degree around subject
- `Handheld` - Shaky, documentary feel
- `Drone shot` - Aerial perspective

### **Lighting Setups**
- `Three-point lighting` - Standard setup
- `Natural daylight` - Outdoor feel
- `Golden hour` - Sunset warmth
- `Neon/cyberpunk` - Artificial colored lights
- `Studio softbox` - Professional even lighting
- `Dramatic rim light` - Backlit silhouette effect

### **Depth of Field**
- `Shallow depth of field` - Blurred background (cinematic)
- `Deep focus` - Everything in focus (documentary)
- `Bokeh effect` - Creamy blurred highlights
