## TYPOGRAPHY

### **Font Pairings:**

**For Thumbnails/Headlines:**
- **Primary:** Montserrat Bold or Poppins Bold
- **Why:** Clean, modern, readable at small sizes
- **Weight:** 700-800 (Bold/Extra Bold)

**For Body/Captions:**
- **Secondary:** Inter or Roboto
- **Why:** Highly legible, neutral, professional
- **Weight:** 400 (Regular)

**For Logo:**
- **Display:** Righteous or Orbitron
- **Why:** Tech/futuristic feel, memorable
- **Style:** Uppercase, letter spacing
