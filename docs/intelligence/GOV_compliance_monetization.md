## COPYRIGHT & AI-GENERATED CONTENT

### **Legal Status (2025):**
- **AI-generated images:** Public domain (no copyright)
- **AI-generated video:** Public domain (no copyright)
- **AI-generated music:** Check tool terms (some claim rights)
- **AI-written scripts:** Can't copyright AI content itself

### **Fair Use for Educational Content:**
- **Showing AI tools:** Legal (educational)
- **Commentary on AI:** Legal (transformative)
- **Reacting to AI content:** Legal (if you add value)
- **Reposting AI art:** Risky (without transformation)

### **Music & SFX:**
- **YouTube Audio Library:** Free, safe to use
- **TikTok Commercial Sounds:** Free to use
- **Instagram Music:** Check "music" sticker (licensed)
- **Epidemic Sound:** Subscription ($15/month)
- **Artlist:** Subscription ($199/year)

## SPONSORSHIP DISCLOSURE (FTC REQUIREMENTS)

### **When Required:**
- **Any paid promotion** (money, free product, service)
- **Affiliate links** (you earn commission)
- **Brand partnerships**
- **Sponsored content**

### **How to Disclose:**
- **YouTube:** Put in description AND video
- **TikTok:** Text overlay + caption + hashtag #ad
- **Instagram:** First line of caption + hashtag #ad

**Example Language:**
- "Thanks to [Brand] for sponsoring this video! #ad"
- "Affiliate links below (I earn a commission)"
- "This video is sponsored by [Brand]"

## TAX & LEGAL STRUCTURE

### **Recommended Structure:**
**LLC (Single-Member LLC)** - Best for creators
- **Liability Protection:** Protects personal assets
- **Tax Benefits:** Pass-through taxation
- **Credibility:** Looks professional to brands
- **Cost:** $50-500 to file (varies by state)

### **Tax Deductions:**
- **Equipment:** Cameras, computers, software
- **Home Office:** Portion of rent/utilities
- **Internet:** Business portion
- **Education:** Courses, books, conferences
- **Travel:** Business-related trips
- **Software:** Adobe, Canva, scheduling tools

### **Estimated Taxes:**
- **Self-Employment Tax:** 15.3% (Social Security + Medicare)
- **Income Tax:** 10-37% (based on bracket)
- **Total:** Set aside 25-30% of revenue

## CONTRACT TEMPLATES

### **Sponsorship Agreement Must Include:**
- **Scope of work** (number of posts, platforms)
- **Deliverables** (video, Reel, Story requirements)
- **Timeline** (posting dates, approval process)
- **Compensation** (payment amount, terms)
- **Exclusivity** (can't work with competitors?)
- **Usage Rights** (how long they can use your content)
- **Approval Rights** (you approve content before posting)
- **Termination** (how either party can end agreement)
