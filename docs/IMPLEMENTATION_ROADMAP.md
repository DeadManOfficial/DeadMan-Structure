### **WEEKS 5-8: ANALYZE & OPTIMIZE**

#### **Weekly Analytics Review**
**Owner:** Analytics Lead
**Every Monday:**
- [ ] Compile previous week's performance
- [ ] Identify top 3 performing videos
- [ ] Identify bottom 3 performing videos
- [ ] Analyze why (watch time, CTR, engagement)
- [ ] Generate insights report
- [ ] Recommend strategy adjustments

#### **Bi-Weekly Strategy Pivots**
**Owner:** Content Commander + Team Leads
**Every 2 Weeks:**
- [ ] Review analytics insights
- [ ] Double down on what works
- [ ] Kill what doesn't
- [ ] Experiment with new formats
- [ ] Adjust content mix
- [ ] Update content calendar

#### **A/B Testing Experiments**
**Test Variables:**
- **Thumbnails:** 3 variations per video
- **Titles:** Emotional vs. descriptive
- **Posting Times:** Morning vs. evening
- **Content Length:** 15s vs. 30s vs. 60s
- **Styles:** Educational vs. entertaining vs. inspiring

**Track Results:**
Document wins in shared spreadsheet, iterate based on data

## ðŸš¨ CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

### **1. CONSISTENCY IS KING**
- Post EVERY day (no exceptions)
- Maintain quality standards
- Stick to content calendar
- Show up for your audience

### **2. DATA-DRIVEN DECISIONS**
- Trust analytics over opinions
- Test everything, keep what works
- Kill losing experiments quickly
- Double down on wins

### **3. SPEED OF EXECUTION**
- Fast iteration = faster learning
- Don't aim for perfection, aim for progress
- Batch similar tasks
- Use templates and systems

### **4. COMMUNITY FIRST**
- Reply to every comment (first 100K followers)
- Build relationships, not just audience
- Show behind the scenes
- Make people feel part of journey

### **5. BRAND CONSISTENCY**
- Maintain visual identity
- Keep consistent posting schedule
- Deliver on your content promise
- Build trust through reliability
