## üîÑ CONTENT PRODUCTION PIPELINE

### **Phase 1: Ideation (Daily)**
**Owner:** Head Prompt Writer + Platform Strategy Lead
**Duration:** 1 hour daily
**Process:**
1. Trend analysis (TikTok Creative Center, YouTube Trends)
2. Competitor research
3. Brainstorming session
4. Select 5 winning concepts
**Output:** 5 content concepts with viral potential score

### **Phase 2: Scripting (Daily)**
**Owner:** Script Writers (A2, A3)
**Duration:** 3 hours daily
**Process:**
1. Long-form writer creates YouTube script (3/day)
2. Short-form writer creates TikTok/IG scripts (10/day)
3. Head Prompt Writer creates Sora 2 prompts
4. Content Commander reviews and approves
**Output:** 3 long-form scripts, 10 short-form scripts, prompts

### **Phase 3: Video Generation (Daily)**
**Owner:** Sora 2 Video Generator (B1)
**Duration:** 4 hours daily
**Process:**
1. Generate Sora 2 videos from approved prompts
2. Iterate on prompts until quality achieved
3. Generate variations (A/B testing)
4. Organize footage in project folders
**Output:** 50 raw video clips

### **Phase 4: Post-Production (Daily)**
**Owner:** Video Editor (B2) + Motion Designer (B3)
**Duration:** 4 hours daily
**Process:**
1. Editor assembles footage
2. Adds music, SFX, transitions
3. Designer creates thumbnails, overlays
4. Format for each platform (YouTube 16:9, TikTok 9:16, IG 4:5)
5. Quality control review
**Output:** 20 polished videos ready for upload

### **Phase 5: Optimization (Daily)**
**Owner:** SEO Specialist (C1) + Caption Writer (C2)
**Duration:** 2 hours daily
**Process:**
1. SEO specialist writes titles, descriptions, tags
2. Caption writer writes captions, hashtags
3. Content Commander final approval
4. Schedule uploads
**Output:** All metadata and captions complete

### **Phase 6: Publishing (Daily)**
**Owner:** Platform Strategy Lead
**Schedule:**
- **YouTube:** 1 long-form + 3 Shorts (Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday)
- **TikTok:** 3-5 videos (daily posting)
- **Instagram:** 2 Reels + 3 Stories (daily posting)
**Output:** Consistent publishing cadence

### **Phase 7: Analysis (Weekly)**
**Owner:** Analytics Lead (C3)
**Duration:** 2 hours weekly
**Process:**
1. Compile all platform analytics
2. Identify top 10% performing content
3. Analyze why it performed
4. Generate insights report
5. Recommend strategy adjustments
**Output:** Weekly performance report + strategy recommendations
